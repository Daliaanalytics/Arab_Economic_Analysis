# Arab_Economic_Analysis

## Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides an economic analysis of selected Arab countries, focusing on key economic indicators such as GDP, GDP per Capita, GNI, Inflation Rate, Unemployment Rate, and GDP Growth. The dashboard helps users compare economic performance across countries and analyze trends over time.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
- Power BI

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Economic Overview

This page provides a general overview of economic performance.

### KPIs
- Maximum GDP (Billion USD)
- Maximum GDP per Capita (Current USD)
- Average Inflation Rate (%)

### Visualizations
- Average GNI by Country
     Compares the average Gross National Income among countries.
- Average GDP per Capita by Country
     Shows the average income level per person across countries.
- Average GDP Growth Over Time
     Line chart displaying GDP growth trends from 2010–2025.

### Page 2: Economic Indicators Analysis

### Visualizations
- Average Inflation by Country
    Compares inflation rates across countries.
- Average Unemployment by Country
    Shows unemployment levels among countries.
- GDP vs GNI
    Scatter plot illustrating the relationship between GDP and GNI.
- Unemployment vs Inflation
    Scatter plot used to explore the relationship between unemployment and inflation rates.

  ## Key Insights
- Saudi Arabia records the highest average GNI among the selected countries.
- United Arab Emirates has the highest GDP per Capita.
- Egypt recorded the highest GDP among the selected countries.
- Egypt experienced the highest average inflation rate among the selected countries.
- Tunisia recorded the highest average unemployment rate.
- A positive relationship exists between GDP and GNI, where countries with larger economies tend to have higher national income.
- The scatter plot does not show a strong direct relationship between unemployment and inflation across all countries.
